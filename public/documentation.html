My Fave Flix App Documentation

This application will enable users to search and find information about their favorite movie including directors, cast, cinematography, and genres. Users will be able to create their own profile with their personal information. and gather and save their list data within their profile. Users will also be able to make their own lists of their favorite movies, directors, and movie genres.
